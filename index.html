Our Primary Goals

Military Primacy

Ensuring strong national defense and security for all Relutians.

Collectivisation of the Economy

Building an economic system that works for everyone, not just the privileged few.

Publicly Owned Food/Media Companies

Ensuring truth in media and food security for all citizens.

Social Housing

Guaranteeing affordable, quality housing as a fundamental right.

Equal Rights for All Proper Relutians

Fighting for justice and equality for every citizen of Relutia.
